about Jashiel
Jashiel has always loved acting. After graduating from the New York's College of Aeronautics with a bachelor's degree in Airport Management, he decided to devote himself full-time to his craft. He began studying at the Penny Templeton Studios in Manhattan and, shortly after, had his broadway debut in Clifton King's "Prayers for the Ghetto." In his free time, he designs underwear and men's accessories out of his home in Brooklyn, NY.
